# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

Shree Ganesh Party Venue & Catering Service

---

# Product Vision

Build a premium event venue and catering platform that helps customers discover services, explore venue options, view event galleries, calculate event costs, and book events online.

The website should generate qualified leads and convert visitors into paying customers.

---

# Business Goals

## Primary Goals

* Increase venue bookings
* Increase catering inquiries
* Increase phone calls
* Increase WhatsApp inquiries
* Build trust through professional branding
* Improve local SEO rankings

---

## Secondary Goals

* Showcase previous events
* Display food menu offerings
* Educate visitors through blogs
* Simplify booking process
* Reduce manual customer support

---
