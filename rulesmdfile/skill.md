## Rules: Do

* Use semantic HTML.
* Use responsive layouts.
* Prioritize booking actions.
* Display trust indicators.
* Include testimonials whenever relevant.
* Use descriptive CTA labels.
* Optimize for mobile first.
* Maintain consistent spacing.
* Use reusable components.
* Ensure forms have validation.

---

## Rules: Don't

* Do not use generic stock imagery.
* Do not hide primary CTAs.
* Do not use auto-playing audio.
* Do not overload pages with animations.
* Do not create cluttered layouts.
* Do not use inconsistent spacing.
* Do not use inaccessible color combinations.

---
